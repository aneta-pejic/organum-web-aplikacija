Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL QUESTIONS

How do I use this web app?

You can create and manage your To-Do List, Notes, and Shopping List all in one place. Just start adding tasks, notes, or shopping items, and they will be saved automatically.

Do I need to create an account?

No, this app works without an account! Your data is stored locally on your device.

Is my data saved if I close the browser?

Yes! Your data is stored in local storage, so it will still be there when you reopen the page. However, if you clear your browser data, it will be lost.

TO-DO LIST

How do I add a new task?

Simply type your task in the input field and click "Add" or just press "Enter" on your keyboard. It will appear in your list instantly.

Can I mark tasks as completed?

Yes! Click on a task to mark it as completed. It will be crossed out to indicate it's done.

How do I delete a task?

Click the "x" next to the task to remove it from your list.

NOTES

How do I create a new note?

Go to the Notes section, type your note, and it will be automatically saved.

Can I edit my notes later?

Yes, just click on a note to edit its text. Changes will be saved automatically.

SHOPPING LIST

How do I add items to my shopping list?

Enter the item name, quantity, and unit, then click "Add". The item will appear on your list.

Can I remove items from the shopping list?

Yes, just click "x" next to the item to remove it.
